Dungeon Unlimited

Dungeon Unlimited
An role playing game set in a Cyberpunk universe written in C++.

With little memories of your past, you awake being taken care by a robot. Your goal is to investigate by talking to people around you, and if need be defend yourself. Looting and dialog choices play a big role in the game.

A complex fighting system has also been built in the game, allowing the player to target specific libs of an enemy for different results.
